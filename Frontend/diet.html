Diet and Exercise Summary

1. Underweight (BMI < 18.5)

Veg Diet:
  • Breakfast: Peanut butter toast + banana + milk
  • Lunch: Paneer curry + rice + salad + curd
  • Evening Snack: Dry fruits + banana shake
  • Dinner: Dal + roti + soya chunks sabzi + ghee
Non-Veg Diet:
  • Breakfast: Boiled eggs + toast + milk
  • Lunch: Chicken curry + rice + salad
  • Evening Snack: Egg sandwich + smoothie
  • Dinner: Fish curry + roti + boiled veggies
Exercise Suggestions:
  • Strength training with light weights
  • Bodyweight exercises: squats, push-ups
  • Yoga for flexibility and appetite boost
  • Avoid intense cardio

2. Normal (BMI= 18.5-24.9)

Veg Diet:
  • Breakfast: Oats + apple + milk
  • Lunch: Rajma + rice + salad
  • Evening Snack: Sprouts chaat + coconut water
  • Dinner: Mixed veg sabji + roti + curd
Non-Veg Diet:
  • Breakfast: Boiled eggs + browned bread + fruit
  • Lunch: Grilled Chicken + Quinoa + salad
  • Evening Snack: Boiled eggs or chicken sandwich
  • Dinner: Fish or egg curry + roti
Exercise Suggestions:
  • 30 - 40 mint moderate workout
  • Brisk walking/ cycling/ swimming
  • Bodyweight workouts (pushups, planks)
  • Yoga or stretching on rest day

3. Overweight (BMI= 25+)

Veg Diet:
  • Breakfast: Vegetable upma + green tea
  • Lunch:Dal + salad + brown rice
  • Evening Snack: Roasted chana + cucumber
  • Dinner:Soup + boiled veggies + 1 roti
Non-Veg Diet:
  • Breakfast: White part of egg + oats + green tea
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken + veggies
  • Evening Snack: Boiled eggs or protine shake
  • Dinner: Fish /chicken + steamed veggies
Exercise Suggestions:
  • Cardio(Walk/jog/cycle)- 30-40 mint daily
  • HIIT (if able) 3x/week
  • Strength training for metabolism
  • Core workouts (planks,mountain climbers)
